Bio


Here is a biography for the artist NUCCI YBL:

NUCCI YBL, born Trust Soko, is a talented Zambian artist and member of the prominent Camp House Gang. With a passion for music and creativity, NUCCI YBL has made a name for himself in the African music scene.

Hailing from Zambia, NUCCI YBL draws inspiration from his roots and culture, blending traditional rhythms with modern styles to create a unique sound. His music often reflects his experiences, thoughts, and emotions, resonating with fans from all walks of life.

As a member of the Camp House Gang, NUCCI YBL has collaborated with other talented artists, pushing the boundaries of innovation and creativity. His dedication to his craft has earned him recognition and admiration from music enthusiasts and industry professionals alike.

With a discography that showcases his growth and versatility, NUCCI YBL continues to produce captivating music that leaves a lasting impression. His artistry is a testament to the power of creativity and the impact it can have on the world.
